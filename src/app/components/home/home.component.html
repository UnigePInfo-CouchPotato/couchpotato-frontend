A problem in the (film-)making

Have you ever been to a pajama party? Or just a party with some friends? Have you ever been stuck thinking of a movie for you to look at? Or fought about what to view next?


You are not alone!

Our solution

Our team has devised a way to minimize conflict while searching for a movie and offers you "CouchPotato".

This free service assists you in choosing a movie to watch when among friends, but also makes it into an event everyone can enjoy.

How does it work?

All you need to do is specify what movie genres you like, join a viewing room, and then vote on the recommended movie that looks the most interesting.

The movie with the most votes is then the one that you will watch.

(And if it doesn't work out, just blame the algorithm!)
